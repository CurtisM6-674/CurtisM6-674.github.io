My research focuses on a pathfinder experiment called the Radar Echo Telescope. This is a novel methodology to detecting the highest energy neutrinos, using an in ice radar technique. Neutrinos play a key role in understanding certain astrophysical processes and are unique in being an observed particle with a variety of unknown properties. I hold a degrees in Physics and Applied Mathematics from Wheaton College (IL).
